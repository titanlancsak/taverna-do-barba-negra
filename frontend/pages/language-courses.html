Free Language Courses

A curated list of free, legitimate resources to learn new languages.

  • Duolingo — Free gamified courses for dozens of languages.
  • BBC Languages — Free structured lessons and guides.
  • Loecsen — Free audio-based phrasebooks.
  • Coursera Language Courses — Free-to-audit university-level courses.
  • Lingoda Free Resources — Grammar guides and exercises.
  • Language Transfer — Free full audio courses (Spanish, Arabic, Greek, and more).
